What is this competition about? +
This is a skill-based competition where the top 3 fastest participants take home a share of the $100,000 prize pool, tax-free. It's all about speed and reaction time—no randomness, just pure skill!
How do I enter the competition? +
Choose your Gateway (Basic, Bronze, Gold, Elite, or Ultimate), pay the entry fee, and you're in! Each Gateway determines the potential prize amount if you rank in the top 3.
How do Gateways work, and how are winnings calculated? +
Gateways determine the prize amount you can win if you rank in the top 3. The prize pool is distributed proportionally based on the Gateway values of the top 3 participants.

**Example:**
  • Participant A chooses Basic Gateway ($30).
  • Participant B chooses Gold Gateway ($100).
  • Participant C chooses Ultimate Gateway ($500).
If these participants rank in the top 3, their winnings are distributed based on their Gateway values:

- Total Gateway value of top 3 = $30 + $100 + $500 = $630.
- Participant A (Basic): ($30 ÷ $630) × $100,000 = **$4,761.90**.
- Participant B (Gold): ($100 ÷ $630) × $100,000 = **$15,873.02**.
- Participant C (Ultimate): ($500 ÷ $630) × $100,000 = **$79,365.08**.

The more valuable your Gateway, the larger your potential winnings!
Can I purchase more than one Gateway? +
Yes! You can purchase multiple Gateways to increase your total Gateway value. The higher your total Gateway value, the greater your share of the prize pool if you rank in the top 3.
Does everyone start at the same time? +
Yes! All participants tap at the same time when the countdown hits zero. The winners are determined purely by reaction time, ensuring fairness for everyone.
What should I do to prepare for the competition? +
Ensure you have a stable internet connection and a fast, responsive device. You'll receive a reminder SMS a day before the competition and the live link on competition day.
Are the winnings tax-free? +
Yes! The $100,000 prize pool is completely tax-free for all winners.
